About Me

Raised in the beautiful mountains of Asheville, NC (home of the Biltmore House and the Grove Park Inn), I studied communications at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (Go Tar Heels). I am currently back to being a Tar Heel studying full stack web development at the UNC Coding Bootcamp, with the goal of working as a junior developer upon completion of the program in early August. When I'm not coding, I'm playing hide-and-go-seek with my two fun and energetic kiddos or tasting something smoked or grilled from my pitmaster husband.
I've spent the majority of my career working for a niche insurance company investigating auto accidents (interesting and rewarding). However, I am truly excited about the new skills I'm learning, developing, and conquering one day at a time in this web development course and I can't wait to see where this journey takes me!
